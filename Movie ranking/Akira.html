Akira is my 2nd favourite movie of all time

I rank Akira as my number two spot

There’s anime, and then there’s Akira. It sits comfortably at number two on my all-time list because it blew the doors off what I thought animation could do.
The world of Neo-Tokyo feels completely alive—polluted, neon-soaked, and dangerous. Plus, the fact that a film from 1988 still looks cleaner, faster, and more visually stunning than half of what comes out today is mind-blowing.
It’s an absolute rush from start to finish.
