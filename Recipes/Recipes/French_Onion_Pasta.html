Hello, this is a recipe for French Onion Pasta

Sections:

  1. Ingredients
  2. Instructions

Ingredients:

  1. 3 large white onions
  2. One whole box of mushrooms
  3. 4 cloves of garlic
  4. 2 small bottle of white wine
  5. 50ml of Olive oil
  6. 2 pints of Beef stock
  7. 1 pack of dried spaghetti
  8. 300ml of double cream
  9. 1 tablespoon of butter
  10. ! pack of grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  • Start by peeling and slicing your onions thinly
  • Into a large pot, add 50ml of Olive oil or enough to coat the bottom of the pot (the oil will stop the butter browning and burning)
  • Add your sliced onion and butter into the pot
  • Slice your mushrooms thinly
  • Crush and dice your garlic cloves
  • Cook down your onions until softened
  • Add your sliced garlic and mushrooms
  • Once the onions, garlic and mushrooms have all softened, add your two small bottles of white wine
  • Keep stirring until the white wine has reduced to remove the alcohol content
  • Once the win has reduced, all 2 pints of beef stock
  • Now add your spaghetti and simmer for 20 - 30 minutes
  • This will cook the spaghetti in the stock
  • Add the cream and stir thoroughly
  • Sprinkly grated Parmesan on top

This recipe will make up roughly 5 portions and can be kept safely in a storage tub and kept in your fridge for up to 5 days
