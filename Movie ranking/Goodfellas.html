Goodfellas is my 5th favourite movie of all time

I rank Goodfellas as my number five spot

Martin Scorsese’s Goodfellas easily locks down the number five spot on my all-time list because it is the absolute gold standard for cinematic pacing.
From the legendary Copacabana tracking shot to the paranoid, cocaine-fueled helicopter frenzy of the final act, the movie never breathes—and neither do you.
It strips away the romanticized mythology of traditional mafia films, replacing it with a hyper-stylized, darkly hilarious, and brutally honest look at the chaotic highs and violent lows of the gangster lifestyle.
Anchored by Ray Liotta’s iconic narration and Joe Pesci’s terrifyingly volatile performance, it’s a masterclass in filmmaking that I can watch on loop and never tire of.
