Tron Legacy is my 4th favourite movie of all time

I rank Tron Legacy as my number four spot

Tron: Legacy easily locks down the number four spot on my all-time list because it’s a masterclass in atmosphere.
It takes the digital world of the original and transforms it into a breathtaking, neon-drenched dystopia.
But what pushes it into my top five is the flawless fusion of sight and sound; the legendary Daft Punk score doesn't just complement the visuals—it drives the entire pulse of the movie.
It’s an immersive, two-hour sensory experience that I can rewatch anytime.
