Hello, this is a recipe for pan seared duck with pomegranate and red wine suace

Sections:

  1. Ingredients
  2. Instructions

Ingredients:

  1. Whole duck breast
  2. 1 bottle of red wine
  3. 4 pomegranates or a tub of pomegranate seeds
  4. 1 tsbp of clear honey
  5. 1 tsbp of Dijon mustard

Instructions

  • If using pomegrantes, cut open with knife and empty seeds. Or if using a tub of seeds, save time. Pour seeds into sauce pan
  • Pour 100ml of red wine into pan
  • Slowly simmer to cook out the alcohol content
  • Pre-heat second pan until smoke appears
  • To cook the perfect pan-seared duck breast with a crisp skin and a tender, medium-rare interior, score the fat in a crosshatch pattern and season liberally with salt. Start the breasts skin-side down in a cold pan over medium-low heat, rendering the fat slowly, then flip to finish
  • Place duck breast onto plate to rest
  • While sauce is cooking, add 1 tsbp of honey
  • Add 1 tsbp of Dijon mustard
  • Slowly stir sauce, watch carefully to ensure that sauce doesn't reduce too much
  • Add as much or little sauce as desired

Enjoy duck with a fruity red wine sauce
