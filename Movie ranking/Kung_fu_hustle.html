Kung Fu Hustle is my 3rd favourite movie of all time

I rank Kung Fu Hustle as my number three spot

Why it's #3: Because where else are you going to see an elderly landlady outrun someone like a literal cartoon character, or musicians using a magical zither to summon ghost warriors?
Kung Fu Hustle takes the absolute spot because it's pure, unadulterated fun.
It’s a gorgeous love letter to classic martial arts cinema wrapped in brilliant comedy, and the axe dance scene alone justifies its place in my top three.
