About Fake Fictional restaurant

Founded in 2012 as a neighborhood passion project, our restaurant was built on a simple, uncompromising vision: to bring people together over exceptional food that actively honors the land it comes from.
Over the years, we have grown into a vibrant culinary community by remaining deeply rooted in our commitment to sustainable, seasonal sourcing. We partner exclusively with local family farms, independent fishmongers, and regenerative growers within a 150-mile radius to ensure every dish tells a story of ethical farming and conscious choices.
From our zero-waste kitchen initiatives to our focus on ocean-friendly seafood, we strive to leave a smaller footprint while delivering a truly transparent dining experience from farm to fork.
