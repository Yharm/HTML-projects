Hello, this is a recipe for Red Thai Peanut Curry and rice or noodles

This recipe is a 3 day process

Sections:

  1. Ingredients
  2. Instructions

Ingredients:

  1. 1 pack of diced steak beef
  2. 1 jar of peanut butter, smooth or crunchy
  3. 3 limes
  4. 1 jar of Thai 7 spice
  5. 3 bunches of spring onions
  6. 1 bottle of light soy sauce
  7. 1 bottle of balsamic vinegar
  8. 1 jar of red thai paste
  9. 2 tins of coconut milk
  10. 1 pack of baby corn
  11. 1 pack of rice or noodles

Instructions

Marinating the beef:

  • Open packet of diced beef and place into a bowl which has a lid
  • Add a 50/50 mixture of soy sauce and balsamic vinegar, enough to cover the beef
  • Add full jar of Thai 7 spice
  • Mix well and leave in fridge for 3 days



Cooking the curry:

  • Begin by chopping up the spring onions and baby corn
  • Place chopped vegetables into large cooking pot
  • Cut limes in half and squeeze into pot
  • Cook veg until spring onions soften
  • Add beef and marinate to pot
  • Add jar of red thai paste, top up half way with water
  • Stir until Beef is cooked
  • Add 3 large tablespoons of peanut
  • Stir until mixed thoroughly
  • Add two tins of coconut milk
  • Stir until combined

    • Serve with rice or noodles of preference and choice
